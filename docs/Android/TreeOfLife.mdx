### RoleSelect

Switch between Captain and Voyager roles and route to appropriate sign in screen or sign up screen. Will detach sign in ribs if on sign up screen and vice versa.

#### Forgot Password

Displays Form where you type in your email and you can request a password recovery email

#### CaptainSignup

Display Captain signup screen fields and call webserviceCallForRegister (Captain version captainRegisterApi)

#### CaptainSignin

Display Captain signin fields and call webserviceCallForLogin

#### VoyagerSignup

#### VoyagerSignup
